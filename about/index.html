About Sam

I am a web developer, IT professional, woodworker and audio engineer. I specialize in custom software development and web hosting, using a variety of open source technologies. I have a passion for the endless pursuit of practical, elegant code.

I have over 10 years of experience building and supporting custom web software for organizations large and small. I have developed everything from youth sports apps to customized CRMs to large-scale customer loyalty programs with users in the millions.

My formative years were spent learning about the value of hard work within our family ranching business. I learned to solve problems and be resourceful, traits I put to use for the benefit of businesses large and small. I believe practical solutions are underrated, and I'm more interested in solving business problems than shiny tech.
